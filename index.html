Uses of Paper

  • Construction Paper: Used for crafts, school projects, and decorations.
  • Tissue Paper: Used for wrapping gifts, packing delicate items, and personal hygiene products.
  • Parchment Paper: Used in baking to prevent sticking, for cooking, and crafting.
